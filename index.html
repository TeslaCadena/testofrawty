Overview

Company Background

The Awty International School in Houston, Texas, is recognized for its global perspective and exceptional education. Committed to fostering academic and cultural growth, Awty offers an international curriculum in a vibrant, diverse community. The school aims to develop responsible global citizens and future leaders.

Target Audience

Awty caters to a global audience, primarily expatriate families, valuing diversity and inclusivity. Marketing communications were provided in multiple languages to accommodate this diverse demographic.

My Role

I managed a wide array of creative and strategic tasks, including designing marketing materials, updating the website, producing campaign videos, and capturing photos. Collaborating with communications and admissions departments, I played a pivotal role in content planning and execution to boost school applications.

Design

Created marketing materials and updated website design

Production

Produced campaign videos and captured photography

Collaboration

Worked with communications and admissions teams

Objectives

Increase Applications

The campaign aimed to attract students aligned with Awty's values, enhancing the school's desirability and selectivity by increasing application numbers.

Enhance Brand Visibility

We communicated the school's core values and educational philosophy through various strategic channels, strengthening its local and international reputation.

Challenges & Solutions

Competition and Cost Perception

To stand out, the campaign highlighted Awty's unique offerings, such as its diverse community and dual Baccalauréat programs. We shared success stories and testimonials to underscore the school's value. Financial aid options were prominently displayed to address cost concerns.

Highlighted unique programs
Shared success stories
Promoted financial aid

COVID-19 Adaptation

In response to the pandemic, Awty transitioned swiftly to remote learning. I contributed to a COVID-19 microsite and organized live streams and webinars to maintain engagement. A creative social media campaign featuring the school mascot kept spirits high.

COVID-19 microsite
Virtual events
Mascot social campaign

Execution

Key enrollment periods were identified to align content with decision-making timelines. A consistent narrative was maintained across all channels, using multimedia production to capture Awty's essence. Materials were multilingual, ensuring broad reach.

Strategic Timing

Aligned with key enrollment decision periods

Multimedia Approach

Videos, photos, and digital content across channels

Multilingual Content

Materials created in multiple languages for global reach

Results

The campaign exceeded expectations, filling enrollment slots and generating a waitlist. It validated Awty's appeal and effectiveness in engaging prospective students and families.
