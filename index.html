Awty International School Admissions Campaign

Overview

Objective: Increase applications and enhance visibility for Houston’s most diverse private institution offering both the French and International Baccalauréat.

Responsibilities: Led creative strategy across web, print, video, and social platforms. Produced multilingual collateral, testimonial videos, and coordinated strategic content rollouts aligned with enrollment cycles.

Challenges

  • Competitive Landscape: Positioned Awty against elite private schools by emphasizing dual diplomas, cultural diversity, and elite university placements.
  • Perceived Cost: Emphasized scholarships and long-term value through testimonials and transparent financial aid messaging.
  • COVID-19: Supported a pivot to remote engagement with a dedicated microsite, livestreams, and mascot-led social media campaigns.

Results

  • Enrollment Goals Surpassed: All seats filled with a waitlist established.
  • Brand Visibility: Increased community engagement and strengthened local and international reputation.

Personal Takeaways

  • Cross-Cultural Communication: Created messaging that resonated with international audiences in multiple languages.
  • Strategic Timing: Aligned campaign rollouts with admissions periods for maximum response.
